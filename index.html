Welcome to Transcend Technologies: Bridging the Digital Divide for Marginalized Communities

Get Started Our Projects

Bridging the digital divide, empowering marginalized communities through Education Technology

Your go to STEM Innovation & Development Hub, Community Library & Platform (The Next Big Thing: Just you wait!)

At Transcend Technologies, we believe that access to technology and education is a fundamental right for all, regardless of background or location. Our mission is to bridge the digital divide among marginalized communities by introducing 21st-century technological literacy tools and equipping learners with essential skills for the future.

Our Impact: Making a Difference

Through our innovative programs and initiatives, we have already made a significant impact in communities across Samburu County and beyond. Here are some key metrics that highlight our achievements:

Join our proposal to expand the following activities to be conducted at the center.

Participants Reached

Almost 1,000 individuals have benefited from our programs and workshops, gaining
access to resources and opportunities
they otherwise wouldn't have had.

Academic Improvement

Students who have participated in our educational programs have seen a noticeable improvement in their grades and test scores, paving the way for brighter futures.

Community Engagement

Our events and workshops have garnered positive
feedback from participants, fostering a sense of community
and collaboration among diverse groups.

Technology Access

We have introduced to remote areas our EdTech resources; Telescope, Arduiono kits, Solar Educational robots, and other technology tools, empowering them to pursue educational and economic opportunities.

Collaboration and Partnerships

Our platform serves as a hub for collaboration and networking within the community. We organize networking events, seminars, and workshops that bring together stakeholders from education, technology, and business sectors. These events will facilitate research, knowledge sharing, partnership building, and the exchange of ideas, fostering a vibrant ecosystem of collaboration and innovation.

Technology Workshops and Seminars

We also organize technology workshops and seminars for community stakeholders like knowledge facilitators, and our members. These sessions focus on integrating technology into teaching practices, utilizing educational software and applications, and staying updated with the latest trends in educational technology. By enhancing the digital skills of knowledge facilitators & learners, we aim to improve the overall quality of education more so in the remote institutions.

Our Portfolio

Our dynamic, game-inspired programs are built on experimental, transformational learning around thought leadership, education technology and address big questions, including: What do you know, Why does it matter, and How can you use it to change the world?

Partners

Working with educational institutions, local governments, NGOs, startup communities, nonprofits, corporations and community organizations across the nation, we scout and train under-represented experts (especially women) to take thought leadership positions in their fields (incubations, networking and much more); we connect them with our international network of high-level media mentors; and we vet and channel the best new ideas and experts directly to media gatekeepers across all platforms.
