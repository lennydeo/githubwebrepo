Services

As a Digital Hub Center in among digital-deserts, we offer a comprehensive range of activities and services designed to meet the specific needs of the community.

Join our proposal to expand the following activities to be conducted at the center.

Access to Tech tools in Education

The Digital Hub Center offers access to computers, internet connectivity, software applications, robotics starter-kits and electronic components(Arduinos). Students & community members will have the opportunity to utilize these resources for research, project work, and personal development. Additionally, we will curate a digital library with educational content, e-books, and online courses to facilitate continuous learning.

Incubation and Mentorship Programs

We are establishing incubation and mentorship programs to support local startups and entrepreneurs in technology-based ventures. The Digital Hub Center will provide access to workspace, mentorship, and resources to foster innovation and entrepreneurial success. We will engage experienced industry professionals who can guide and support aspiring entrepreneurs throughout their journey.

Collaboration and Networking Opportunities

The Digital Hub Center will serve as a hub for collaboration and networking within the community. We will organize networking events, seminars, and workshops that bring together stakeholders from education, technology, and business sectors. These events will facilitate knowledge sharing, partnership building, and the exchange of ideas, fostering a vibrant ecosystem of collaboration and innovation.

Technology Workshops and Seminars

The Digital Hub Center will organize technology workshops and seminars for teachers, educators, and professionals. These sessions will focus on integrating technology into teaching practices, utilizing educational software and applications, and staying updated with the latest trends in educational technology. By enhancing the digital skills of educators, we aim to improve the overall quality of education in the county.

Clients

Working with educational institutions, local governments, NGOs, startup communities, nonprofits, corporations and community organizations across the nation, we scout and train under-represented experts (especially women) to take thought leadership positions in their fields (incubations, networking and much more); we connect them with our international network of high-level media mentors; and we vet and channel the best new ideas and experts directly to media gatekeepers across all platforms.
